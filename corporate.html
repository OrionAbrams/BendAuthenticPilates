INCENTIVES FOR CORPORATIONS
—

Corporate bookings are available. Whether your organization is small or large, we can create a wellness program to meet your organizations needs.

We run the following programs:
• Pilates lunchtime matwork classes
• Nutrition cooking demonstrations for healthy, breakfast, lunch & dinner options.
• Breakfast smoothies made simple – all the necessary components for a healthy, energizing start to your work day.
• Corporate massage sessions. At desk or in private room.
• Hand or foot reflexology mini treatments.
• Coaching for work/life balance
• Nutrition for stress in the workplace
• Stretch & relax sessions
• Personalized nutrition & coaching, so your staff can excel in their roles

Contact Isabelle at bendauthenticpilates@gmail.com to discuss your organizations needs & pricing options.
